About Us

making New India, 'Aatma Nirbhar Bharat'

Karya Bharath is a one-stop digital marketplace where job seekers including the blue-collar workforce and employers can fulfill their needs of employment, vetting, referrals, upskilling, market insights, and knowledge management. Conceptualized around the needs of the industry, human resources, and the skill ecosystem Karya Bharath, would not only enhance the lives of weaker societies but also contribute to the larger vision of our Prime Minister, in making New India, 'Aatma Nirbhar Bharat'.
